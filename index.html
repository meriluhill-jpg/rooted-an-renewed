My Story

This wasn't the story
I planned.

Several years ago, I found myself walking through something I never expected as a parent. A child I deeply love chose distance from our family. What began as difficult conversations eventually became silence.

Like many of you, my first instinct was to fix it. I replayed conversations. I searched for answers. I questioned my decisions. I carried a weight of grief I didn't know how to put into words.

I wanted restoration. I wanted reconciliation. I wanted the relationship back.

But over time, God began showing me something I wasn't looking for.

Before He restored a relationship — He wanted to restore my heart.

This is the space where I share what that journey has looked like. The honest parts. The hard parts. The parts where God showed up in ways I didn't expect and wouldn't have chosen — but can't imagine having missed.

If you're a parent in the waiting, you belong here.

The Study

Restoration

A 12-Week Study for the Parent in Waiting

This study was born from a season of waiting. It is designed for the parent who is left — the one at the window — and built on one conviction: the work God wants to do in you while you wait is not a consolation prize. It is the thing itself.

Each week includes video teaching, daily study questions, commentary, and a creative reflection day. Move at whatever pace allows you to be honest rather than efficient.

About Merilu

Writer. Podcast Host.
Mother. In the waiting.

Merilu Hill is a writer, podcast host, Bible study leader, wife, and mother of four. She is the host of Rooted & Renewed, where she shares honest conversations about faith, growth, healing, and learning to walk with God in real life.

Her writing is shaped by Scripture, lived experience, and a deep belief that God does some of His most tender work in hidden places.

She lives in Beaver, Pennsylvania, with her husband and family.
